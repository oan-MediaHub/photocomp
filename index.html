Oshwal Academy Nairobi - Senior High

Media Hub Photography Competition

Competition Information

1. Eligibility:

Open to all students at Oshwal Academy Nairobi Senior High.

2. Themes:

  • Astrophotography
  • Vehicles
  • Natural Landscapes

3. Entry Requirements:

  • Participants can submit up to 3 entries per category.
  • File size: Maximum of 10MB.
  • File format: JPEG or PNG.
  • Submissions accepted via links provided for each section.

4. Originality:

  • All entries must be the original work of the participant.
  • Digital manipulation is allowed, but excessive alterations should be disclosed.

5. Timeline:

  • Submission period: 15th February - 29th February
  • Deadline for submissions: 29th Februrary 2024 - 11:59PM
  • Judging period: 4th March - 7th March

6. Judging Criteria:

  • Creativity and Originality
  • Technical Excellence
  • Composition
  • Artistic merit
  • Emotional Impact

7. Usage Rights:

  • Participants retain copyright but grant organizers the right to display, publish, and promote entries for non-commercial purposes.
  • Any photograph containing the property of another person (face, vehicle, etc.) must provide a written consent of use when asked for.

8. Disqualification Criteria:

  • Violation of rules.
  • Plagiarism.
  • Inappropriate content.
